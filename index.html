Scientists have studied how the brain learns and found that regular practice is one of the most effective ways to improve memory and skills. When we repeat an activity, the brain builds stronger connections between neurons. This process, called "neuroplasticity", helps information move faster and more easily in the brain.

Research also shows that short study sessions are better than long ones. Instead of studying for many hours at once, students should review information in smaller periods over several days. This method, known as "spaced learning," allows the brain to store knowledge more effectively.

Another important factor is sleep. During sleep, the brain organizes and strengthens what we have learned during the day. Students who do not get enough sleep often find it harder to concentrate and remember information.

Failure also plays a role in learning. When students make mistakes and correct them, the brain becomes more active and improves its understanding. This shows that learning is not only about getting the right answer but also about the process of thinking and trying again.

By using these methods, students can study smarter and achieve better results.

📖 Phần 1 – Reading (Q1–6) ▼

Q1 – D: Đoạn văn nêu các phương pháp học hiệu quả dựa trên khoa học (neuroplasticity, spaced learning, sleep, failure). Đáp án D tóm tắt đúng ý chính.
Q2 – A: Đoạn 1: "the brain builds stronger connections between neurons" – đây là nội dung của neuroplasticity.
Q3 – D: Bài đề xuất học ngắn, ngủ đủ giấc, ôn thường xuyên; "Studying many hours without breaks" (D) KHÔNG được đề cập là cách cải thiện mà ngược lại.
Q4 – B: "This" ở đoạn 4 thay thế cho ý "When students make mistakes and correct them, the brain becomes more active" → the idea that mistakes help learning.
Q5 – D: "organizes" = sắp xếp → gần nghĩa nhất với arranges (D).
Q6 – C: "effectively" (hiệu quả) ↔ trái nghĩa: poorly (C) = kém hiệu quả.

📖 Phần 2 – Grammar (Q7–14) ▼

Q7 – D: "As soon as" = ngay khi → mệnh đề thời gian chỉ hành động xảy ra liền ngay.
Q8 – D: Cấu trúc such + a/an + adj + noun: "such an interesting book".
Q9 – A: "because of" + danh từ/cụm danh từ ("the heavy rain"). "Because" đi với mệnh đề.
Q10 – B: Cấu trúc so sánh kép: The more…, the more… → "the more confident".
Q11 – B: "By the time" + quá khứ đơn → mệnh đề chính dùng quá khứ hoàn thành: arrived / had started.
Q12 – B: Đơn vị đo lường của lettuce (rau diếp/cải bắp): a head of.
Q13 – D: "detest" theo sau là V-ing: getting.
Q14 – D: Kristine nhận lời giúp đỡ, câu phù hợp nhất: "That's very kind of you."

📖 Phần 3 – Rewrite (Q15–16) ▼

Q15 – C: "so that he won't fail" → "in order not to fail" (B sai vì "in order to not" không chuẩn).
Q16 – C: Reported speech → Direct: "would" trong lời gián tiếp trở thành "will" trong lời trực tiếp: "I will pick you up after school."

📖 Phần 4 – Signs & Notices (Q17–18) ▼

📖 Phần 5 – Cloze (Announcement) Q19–22 ▼
